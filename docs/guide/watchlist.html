# Indices

Indices: $SPY → Tracks the 500 largest companies in the US. Overall picture of the market. $QQQ → Tracks NASDAQ 100, largely tech based overall picture. $VIX → Expectation of future volatility, basically it's a hedge or trades inversely from SPY. If Vix goes up, SPY comes down and vice versa. Times of great uncertainty causes expected future volatility Uncertainty causes fear / bearish markets (good if VIX is low) $IWM → Tracks the Russell 2000 Index which measures small cap performance in the US. $AMC/$GME → I use $AMC and $GME as indexes basically that paint a picture of squeeze plays. If these two are hot I will go to WSB names: $SNDL, $CLOV, $PLTR, $SPRT, $BB stocks with high short % Staples: (One main for each major sector) $TSLA → EV’s $AMZN → Retail $AAPL → 18% of the S&P, carries the indices $GOOGL/$FB → Tech $MRNA → Vaccines $AMD + $NVDA → Semiconductors $BABA → China $ROKU → Consumer services $COIN → Bitcoin
